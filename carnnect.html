Understand - Empathize

Needfinding with Drivers

Goal :

Interview 8 commuters (3 students and 5 industry workers) to learn about drivers' behaviors in traffic.
What do they really do sitting in traffic?

What we learned :

All of our interviewers expressed concern with the amount of time wasted in traffic and desire to reduce their time spent in traffic. However, everyone had unique ways to mitigate stress in traffic. For example, stretching, smoking, listening to music, etc. However, everyone expressed that being in traffic with a companion greatly reduces their boredom in traffic.

Implement - Convergent Ideation

Feasibility is very Important

Goal :

Narrow these wild ideas into ones that are feasible. Think of different solutions in combined dimensions of the problem space.

Outcome :

After considering all possible solutions, our team decided to expand on the idea presented on storyboard #3 because stakeholders expressed they feel less stressful when they were interaction with other people in traffic. Therefore, we decided to create a car application that allows the driver to connect with nearby drivers stuck in traffic to partake in various activities such as debate/discussion, music sharing, and caraoke battle. Our team decided to name this idea "Carnnect".

Test - Pilot Study

Goal :

Although testing our design wasn't a requirement for this course, our team decided to test the feasibility of the paper prototypes in real traffic conditions through a pilot study.

What worked :

The different activities keep the driver entertained during traffic.

What we learned :

We learned that safety requirements are crucial for Carnnect. Since driving is a simple cognitive task, we learned that Carnnect has to be easy to navigate with many safety precautions. Furthermore, all drivers have different driving behavior. We learned we have to consider the different personality traits of each driver.

Pilot study with paper prototype

What we added

Safety Features :

We included safety features that alerts the user if the vehicle goes over 15 miles per hour and automatically disconnects if the speed exceeds 25 miles per hours. Carnnect is also voice activated to prevent distraction for the user while driving.

Different Modes :

We also added a private mode for introverted individuals who want to listen, but not actively engage in the discussion.
